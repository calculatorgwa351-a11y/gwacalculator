Latin Honors Policy

Graduating students who meet the following academic requirements shall be awarded Latin Honors:

Summa Cum Laude
1.00 - 1.20
Magna Cum Laude
1.21 - 1.45
Cum Laude
1.46 - 1.75

General Requirements:

  • Must have completed the degree within the prescribed period.
  • No failing grade (5.0) in any academic or non-academic subject.
  • No grade lower than 2.5 in any subject (including elective/minor).
  • Must have carried a regular load (min. 15 units) every semester.

Official Grading Scale

Numerical Grade Percentage Equiv. Description
1.0 - 1.2 96 - 100% Excellent
1.3 - 1.5 91 - 95% Very Good
1.6 - 2.5 81 - 90% Good / Satisfactory
2.6 - 3.0 75 - 80% Fair / Passing
5.0 Below 75% Failure

Retention Policy

Students must maintain a minimum weighted average of 2.5 or better in major subjects to stay in highly technical programs (Engineering, Technology).

Warning Status

Failure in 25% of academic load.

Probation

Failure in 50% of academic load.
